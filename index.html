Privacy Policy

SpikeSaver for Tesco — Chrome Extension • Last updated: 6 February 2025

Summary: SpikeSaver does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information stays on your device.

What SpikeSaver Does

SpikeSaver is a browser extension that analyses nutrition information displayed on Tesco.com product pages. It scores products against health goals you configure, highlights allergens, and provides shopping recommendations — all within your browser.

Data We Collect

None. SpikeSaver does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking pixels, and no third-party services.

Data Stored Locally

SpikeSaver uses your browser's built-in storage (chrome.storage.local) to save:

  • Your health goals and dietary preferences (configured during setup)
  • Allergen alert settings
  • A count of products analysed (for the "Products Analysed Today" counter)
  • Extension settings and wizard completion status

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by removing the extension or clearing extension data in Chrome settings.

Website Access

SpikeSaver requires access to www.tesco.com to read product nutrition information displayed on the page. It does not:

  • Access any other websites
  • Read or modify your Tesco account information
  • Access your basket, payment details, or order history
  • Inject advertising or affiliate links

Permissions Explained

  • storage — To save your preferences and settings locally on your device
  • activeTab — To read nutrition data from the Tesco product page you are viewing
  • host_permissions (tesco.com) — To run the nutrition analyser on Tesco grocery pages

Third-Party Services

SpikeSaver does not use any third-party services, APIs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

SpikeSaver does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Since no data is collected, material changes are unlikely.
